About Me

A closer look at my interests, how I approach design, and the skills I’m building as an IT student.

I’m currently in my second year of a Bachelor of Information Technology at the University of Canberra, with a strong interest in cybersecurity, health technology, and artificial intelligence.

I’m particularly interested in how emerging technologies can be used to solve real-world problems, especially in healthcare and secure digital systems where design decisions can have a real impact on people’s lives.

I enjoy taking ideas and turning them into structured, thoughtful designs through user research, design thinking, prototyping, and system mapping.
